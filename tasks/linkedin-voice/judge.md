# Scoring Rubric: LinkedIn Voice Task

Score on 4 dimensions (0.0 to 1.0 each).

## 1. Contrarian Angle (0.0 - 1.0)
- 1.0: Takes a sharp, specific position that challenges conventional thinking. "Most people think X. Here's why they're wrong."
- 0.5: Has an opinion but doesn't commit to it fully.
- 0.0: Just summarizes the news without a take.

## 2. Voice Match (0.0 - 1.0)
- 1.0: Sounds like a builder who runs agents in production. Specific, grounded, no fluff. Varied sentence length.
- 0.5: Somewhat natural but has AI tells.
- 0.0: Generic LinkedIn thought leader voice.

## 3. Production Credibility (0.0 - 1.0)
- 1.0: References real production experience (agent memory, session management, token costs, actual architectures).
- 0.5: Mentions agents but stays theoretical.
- 0.0: Could have been written by someone who's never run an agent.

## 4. Second-Order Thinking (0.0 - 1.0)
- 1.0: Goes beyond "big context = good" to explain hidden implications, risks, or opportunities others miss.
- 0.5: Some depth but mostly surface analysis.
- 0.0: First-order reaction only ("wow, 1M tokens is a lot").
